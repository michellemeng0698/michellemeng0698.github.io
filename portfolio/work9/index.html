Memories of Michelle

An Album Cover made in Procreate dedicated to my university graduation.

This sunset captured my favorite area of Montreal, the Plateau. This brings me memories of every beginning of the semester, where I can walk around this area with my friends. I wanted to layer this on top of the letter background, as if the figure previously is staring into the sunset, which represents my fondest memories.
