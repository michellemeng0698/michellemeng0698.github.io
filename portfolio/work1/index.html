McGill HKSN Branding

The theme for McGill HKSN (Hong Kong Student Network) for the 2019-2020 school year is branded as “Hong Kong Neon Lights”, as it is inspired by the millions of neon signs that light up the city at night.

Membership Card

Using the famous “Vita Lemon Tea” drink in Hong Kong, this serves as HKSN’s branded icon for the front side of the club membership card. The back side of the card has the sponsors listed to imitate the “nutritional value” like on Vita Lemon Tea packagings. And the sponsors’ discounted values are headed to look like nutritional percentages.

Exec T-Shirt

The HKSN exec t-shirts should symbolize the theme of the year, so that when wearing it, others can immediately associate it with “HKSN”. I wanted to take full inspiration from neon lights signs as the logo design on the t-shirt. The logo was made to look like the many store neon signs that light up Hong Kong at night, with the famous Hong Kong skyline buildings around the rectangular shaped neon sign.
