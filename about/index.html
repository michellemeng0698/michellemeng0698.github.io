About me

I strive to combine my knowledge in Marketing with my interests in design & illustration to become a compelling storyteller of brands.

Moving to new places from a young age has taught me the best way to survive is to try to blend in like a chameleon. But unfortunately, that’s never the case. Moving from places to places adds to my identity which makes it hard to disguise as an insider at any place. I used to be frustrated by this, but now I have grown to love each new perspective that I’ve gain as an outsider.

So what, if I can’t be an insider of anywhere? I get to be an outsider of everywhere.

As an outsider, I just want to be a storyteller who uses colourful ideas & designs to illustrate unique stories for everyone.
